The 2012 Tour

Synthetica

As most of you know, we just released our new album 'Synthetica.' This is first new album since we released 'Fantasies.' The new album features 11 new tracks and comes with a companion album called 'Synthetica Reflections.' You can pick up our album on Amazon, through iTunes and can listen to the album on Spotify. Our 2012 tour is moving forward as we check off our US date before returning to Canada. We recently played in Pittsburgh at Stage AE. During the day, we headed over to the Andy Warhol Museum and left four tickets for a lucky fan to grab and posted a picture of their location on facebook. We are headed to Philly next so everyone should keep tabs on our page to see if you can score some free tickets to our show.
