Star Wars: The Phantom Menace

This review released in 2009, was the review that really got our name out there. We've made reviews in the past, but this is the one that really jump started our business. Yes, this review is seventy minutes long, but we feel it's worth it. When this film was released in 1999, fans had been waiting over a decade for a new Star Wars fil, but when they felt the theater something was wrong and no one could put there finger on what it was. That's why we're here to help. The awfulness of the Star Wars prequels can't be wrapped up in any one scene or choice Lucas made. It's really the whole film and that's why our reviews are so long.
